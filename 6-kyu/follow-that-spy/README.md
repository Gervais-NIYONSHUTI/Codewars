<h2><a href=https://www.codewars.com/kata/5899a4b1a6648906fe000113/train/java target="_blank">Follow that Spy</a></h2><h3>6 kyu</h3><p>We are diligently pursuing our elusive operative, <strong>Matthew Knight</strong>, who also goes by the alias <strong>Roy Miller</strong>. He employs a nomadic lifestyle to evade detection, constantly moving from one location to another, with each of his journeys following a perplexing and non-standard sequence of <strong>itineraries</strong>. Our mission is to <em>decipher the routes</em> he will undertake during each of his voyages.</p><h2 id="task">Task</h2><p>You've been provided with an array of itinerary <code>routes</code>, decipher the <strong>precise destinations</strong> he will visit in the correct sequence according to his meticulously planned itineraries.</p><h2 id="example">Example</h2><p>Based on the provided routes:</p><pre><code>[ [USA, BRA], [JPN, PHL], [BRA, UAE], [UAE, JPN] ]</code></pre><p>The correct sequence of destinations is:</p><pre><code>"USA, BRA, UAE, JPN, PHL"</code></pre><p><strong>Note:</strong> </p><ul><li>You can safely assume that there will be <em>no duplicate locations</em> with distinct <code>routes</code>.</li><li>All <code>routes</code> provided will have <em>non-empty itineraries</em>.</li><li>There will always be <em>at least one (1)</em> route connecting one waypoint to another.</li></ul>
